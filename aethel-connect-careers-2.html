About the Organisation

Aethel Connect is a fixed wireless internet provider serving homes and businesses across Gauteng and Bloemfontein. We are committed to closing the connectivity gap in underserved South African communities.

As we expand our footprint, we are recruiting five motivated individuals to represent our brand on the ground and help more households and businesses get connected.

Position Summary

The Connectivity Consultant is a customer-facing, commission-based role. You will introduce Aethel Connect's fixed wireless internet packages to potential customers in your area, answer basic product questions, and refer interested clients to our support team for application processing and installation. You are not responsible for technical, billing, or administrative tasks — your role is to engage, explain, and refer.

Key Responsibilities

  • Promote Aethel Connect fixed wireless internet packages to residential and business customers in your local area.
  • Provide clear, accurate explanations of available packages, pricing, and coverage to prospective clients.
  • Identify interested customers and direct them to the Aethel Connect support line for coverage checks, application assistance, and installation scheduling.
  • Maintain an up-to-date record of all leads generated and customers contacted.
  • Submit a written activity report every Monday morning, detailing the number of individuals approached, leads generated, and any follow-up actions required.
  • Represent Aethel Connect professionally and in accordance with the company's values at all times.

Remuneration

This is a commission-based position. Consultants earn a recurring monthly commission for every client they successfully refer, for as long as that client remains an active subscriber.

Commission becomes active upon completion of the client's installation. Payouts are processed monthly. There is no cap on the number of clients you may refer — your earning potential grows directly with your active client base.

Minimum Requirements

There are no formal educational or experience requirements for this position. We welcome applications from all backgrounds. The following attributes are, however, essential:

  • Must be legally eligible to work in South Africa. South African citizens will be given preference in the selection process.
  • Residing in Gauteng or Bloemfontein — applicants outside these areas will not be considered.
  • Confident communicator with the ability to engage members of the public in a professional and friendly manner.
  • Self-motivated and able to work independently without daily supervision.
  • Able to maintain basic written records and submit a weekly report.
  • Access to a smartphone for communication and reporting purposes.

Training & Support

  • Full product training provided prior to commencement — no prior knowledge of internet services is required.
  • Marketing materials, scripts, and digital assets provided to support your sales conversations.
  • Ongoing support from the Aethel Connect team for product queries and client escalations.
